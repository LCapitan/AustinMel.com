Portrait

Subject: Joelle Nawrocki

Let me start by just expressing how much I appreciate that my girlfriend is always willing to be my beautiful model. I took this picture during the fall in Canton, CT. This photo actually was not posed, which I love. To me, candid photos are more genuine in every way. Poses aren't bad, but not everyone is comfortable posing. After years of editing photos that look forced, Ive learned to discover where people are most comfortable in front of the camera within the first few moments of a photo shoot. Some, like Joelle, are naturals through the lens, others just need to find some comfort in their own skin. One of the things I love about photography, is that I can draw peoples' attention to and from certain areas of a photo. For example, in this photo, I wanted everyone to focus on only Joelle.
