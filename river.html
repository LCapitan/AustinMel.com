Portrait

Subject: Joelle Nawrocki

Here's my lady again, beautiful as ever. Joelle's father has a house down on the river that we visit pretty often, so I brought the camera one day to get a few pictures. Oddly enough, what I love about this photo is actually the spacing. I set Joelle up in the middle third of the photo and left the top third of the photo open as well. This really opens up the image and leaves a lot to the imagination, as the woods do for many people.
