Grace Academy

My girlfriend, Joelle - another talented photographer and editor - and I had the opportunity to film and produce this video for Grace Academy. Grace Academy is a school for young women, located in Hartford, CT. It was a privilage to be able to go and meet all the beautiful, bright students that make this school, and made this video amazing. The school focuses on accepting girls from all around the city of Hartford, and giving them the opportunity to receive a great education. The students and staff treated us like their own while we were there filming, and we will always be very grateful for that. I can't wait to work with them more over the years!
