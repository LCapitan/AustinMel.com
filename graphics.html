The day I stepped into my first Graphic Design class and was introduced to Photoshop was, and will always be, one of the greatest days of my life. I'll never forget Mr. Eisner showing us all of the things you could do with the program, I just remember thinking to myself, "Art is no longer just a hobby for me." As the class went on and we began to cover some more complex features, I was just taken back by the technology and the amount of things I could do with it. So of course, since I was young, I started drawing the only thing that my mind could thing to design...Spider-Man. Normally, some people would grow out that phase, I have not. I still love to draw him on my iPad and measure my progress.

If you don't like Spider-Man, that's okay! I'll still happily design whatever you need, no matter how complex. From custom logos, to whole spreadsheets of icons or a brochure of brand standards, I can get you what you need, and exactly what you want.
